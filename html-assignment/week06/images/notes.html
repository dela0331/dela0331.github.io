Some things I've learned so far:

<em>
Used for emphasis on certain text. Important to ensure proper tone for screen readers.
<abbr>
Used to abbreviate certain info.
<strong>
Used on content of strong importance. May be used in many contexts.
<blockquote>
Used to indicate that the enclosed text is an extendended quotation. - developer.mozilla.org
<img>
Used to place images on webpage.
